Intervals can be thought of as months or 30/day increments.

  For this blockchain, the following values are set:
Initial Supply : 100,000,000 VKGs/NULS
Inflation begins in intervals: 24 (2 years)
Inflation tokens per interval: 5,000,000 / 12 (416,666.66) VKGs or NULs
Inflation is turned off when inflation reaches: 210,000,000 VKGs/NULS
De-inflation ratio is: 0.004
De-inflation and inflation begin at the same time.
